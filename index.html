About Us

Gymshark

We're Gymshark. We exist to unite the community.


It’s not just about the goals we set, it’s about what we do to reach them. We might train in different ways and aim for different things, but sweat is what connects us. We’re a group of individuals who get that we go further when we go together.

It all started back in 2012, in a garage in Birmingham, UK, with a sewing machine, a screen printer, and some big dreams. Now, we make the gear that helps you chase your best the clothes you train in, the content that keeps you motivated, and the community that’s with you every step of the way.
